Our Idea

Our Idea is to prevent the amount of money, time, fuel and infrastructure spent on collecting toll amount on highways and airport toll booths. This will be accomplished by deploying more full time surveillance cameras along the highways and the toll booth part of the airport. We do not aim to remove the checkpoint in airport area as it has a very high importance seccurity wise, but to install which capture the entry time and exit time of the vehicle and bill them accordingly.

We also aim to reduce car theft, speeding and faster catching of criminals by deploying the same algorithm, of number plate recognition to city cameras which would be directly linked with traffic police thereby creating a full networked database of all the cars in the country.

The next would be to integrate out services with the local RTO and transfer all the database to the cloud making it mandatory to have an account with our service.

How It Works - Background

Car Number Plate is captured

Detection of number plate and and number written on it will de done using opencv module.
